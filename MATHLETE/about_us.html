The Mathlete Institution is started with the aim of helping students who love to explore more of Mathematics. This institution was establised on 5 may 2022 at puthenchira, which can help the rural students a helping hand for mathematics.

We provide admission for those students who take to dive into the depth of mathematics. Students who wish to take admission into our institute should complete their under graduation in mathematics or must studied mathematics as their complementary course atleast for two years.

This institution aims at helping students who have immense love toward mathematics and will flourish it into its depth in upcoming years retaining the aim.

The college have amply library facilities and a wide playground which helps students for their dreams.

Our institution provides seven courses on mathematics which holds duration of six months to two years that can help the students get a depthful knowledge of mathematics. On completing the course we provide paid internships which carries period of three months to six months. This will explore students into the job sector of mathematics and they will get work experience. A small description of courses provided is given below.

Basic Mathematics

This course will cover basic level of mathematics which makes you capable of gaining knowledge on basics of mathematics you need after under graduate completion.This course is designed also for the quick cover of the entire basics you studied so far. Duration of this course is six months and will have paid internships of three months. The fee is only six thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Geometry

This course will cover the geometric aspects of mathematics which makes you capable of gaining knowledge on the you need on the geometrical arena of mathematics. Duration of this course is eight months and will have paid internships of four months. The fee is only twelve thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Calculus

This course will cover the calculus area mathematics which makes you capable of gaining knowledge on the aspect of calculus. Duration of this course is nine months and will have paid internships of four months. The fee is only ten thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Trigonometry

This course will cover the trigonometry of mathematics which makes you capable of gaining knowledge on the trigonometry. Duration of this course is ten months and will have paid internships of five months. The fee is only thirteen thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Basic Mathematics and Calculus

This course comes like a combination of Basic Mathematics and Calculus. Students Who wish to have make strong base on the calculus can opt this course. Duration of this course is one year and will have paid internships of six months. The fee is only twenty thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Geometry and Trigonomtry

This course is a combination of both Trigonometry and Geometry. Students who have immense love towards the both can opt this course. Duration of this course one year and will have paid internships of six months. The fee is only twenty five thousand rupees for the entire course duration.

Advanced Mathematics

This course will combine all four branches of mathematics. Students who wish to have knoweledge on the all four branches can opt this course. Duration of this course is two years and will have paid internships of one year. The fee is only fourty five thousand rupees for the entire course duration.
